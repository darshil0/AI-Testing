# Test Scenarios: Translation and Summarization

This document outlines the test scenarios for evaluating an AI model's ability to perform translation and summarization tasks.

## Scenario 1: Translation

**Objective:** To assess the model's ability to accurately translate a sentence from English to French.

**Test Case:** `test_translation.txt`

**Description:** This test case contains a simple English sentence. The model is expected to provide a grammatically correct and contextually accurate translation in French.

**Evaluation Criteria:**
-   Grammatical correctness of the translated text.
-    preservation of the original meaning.

## Scenario 2: Summarization

**Objective:** To assess the model's ability to summarize a paragraph into a single, concise sentence.

**Test Case:** `test_summarization.txt`

**Description:** This test case contains a paragraph of text. The model is expected to generate a single-sentence summary that captures the main idea of the paragraph.

**Evaluation Criteria:**
-   The summary should be a single sentence.
-   The summary should accurately reflect the main points of the paragraph.
-   The summary should be grammatically correct and fluent.
